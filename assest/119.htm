    Overview

    Vivid Visuals

    Offering a 3840x2160 pixel resolution, the Croma CREL050USA024601 50-inch Ultra HD4K LED TV delivers a vivid and detailed visual experience. Moreover, the TV’s refresh rate of up to 60Hz allows you to enjoy action-packed scenes without the distraction of motion blur.


    Vivid Picture Engine

    You can enjoy smooth and responsive performance on this smart TV, all thanks to its advanced Vivid Picture Engine picture processor. It can enhance TV performance, ensuring vivid colours, sharp details, and smooth motion.


    Smooth Visuals

    With its rapid 9.5ms response time, this television provides a seamless gaming experience, enabling you to react quickly to in-game events.


    20W Built-In Speakers

    Housing 20W built-in speakers, this 50-inch TV offers you a rich and powerful sound output to match the high-quality visuals.


    Dolby Audio

    With high-quality Dolby Audio technology, this TV offers realistic and well-balanced audio performance.


    WebOS

    This Croma smart TV’s operating system is user-friendly, making it hassle-free to navigate and access apps, streaming services, and settings. What’s more, this Wi-Fi TV brings you a variety of entertaining apps, including Netflix, Prime Video, YouTube, and Disney+ Hotstar.


    Google Assistant

    Supporting Google Assistant, this TV lets you search for content, adjust settings, and even ask questions using voice commands, adding a new level of convenience and interactivity to your entertainment experience.


    Home Dashboard

    With the user-friendly home dashboard on your TV, you can conveniently control all your connected smart devices from one big screen. You can create your personalised daily routines effortlessly with the assistance of the magic remote.


    Intelligent Motion Remote

    Experience the magic of the Bluetooth-based intelligent motion remote for your TV. This remote allows you to control your TV or set-top box effortlessly from any corner of the room, eliminating the need to stand directly in front of the device.


    Connect Your Entertainment World

    This smart TV comes with three HDMI ports and two USB ports, providing you with versatile connectivity options for various external devices, such as gaming consoles, Blu-ray players, soundbars, and USB drives.
